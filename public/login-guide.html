FORMENTERA

Work Order App — Login Guide

For employees with a @formenteraops.com email

1 Open the App

Open your web browser (Chrome, Safari, or Edge) on your phone or computer and go to:

formentera-workorder.vercel.app
Tip: On your phone, you can add this to your home screen for quick access. In Safari, tap the share icon and select "Add to Home Screen." In Chrome, tap the menu (three dots) and select "Add to Home Screen."
2 Tap "Sign in with Microsoft"

On the login screen, tap the "Sign in with Microsoft" button. You do not need to type in the email/password fields above it.

3 Enter Your Microsoft Credentials

You will be redirected to the Microsoft login page. Enter your @formenteraops.com email address and your Microsoft password.

This is the same email and password you use for Outlook, Teams, and other Microsoft apps.

4 Approve the Sign-In Request

Microsoft will send a verification request to your Outlook mobile app. You will see a screen with a number (e.g. 49).

Open the Outlook app on your phone and enter the number shown on the screen to approve the sign-in.

Don't see the notification? Swipe down to refresh the Outlook app. If you still don't receive it, tap "I can't use my Outlook mobile app right now" for alternative verification options.
5 "Stay Signed In?" — Tap Yes

After approval, Microsoft will ask "Stay signed in?". Tap Yes so you don't have to repeat this process every time you open the app.

6 You're In

You will be redirected to the Work Order App home screen. From here you can:

  • Submit maintenance tickets
  • View and manage your tickets
  • See dashboards and analytics
Having trouble?
If you're unable to log in or don't see your tickets after signing in, contact Alex Benavides for assistance.
